  • Use the Arrow Keys to navigate Pac-Man through the maze
  • Eat all the pac-dots to advance to the next level
  • Avoid the ghosts - they will chase you!
  • Eat power pellets to temporarily turn the ghosts blue and vulnerable
  • Eat blue ghosts for extra points
  • Use the tunnels on the left and right to teleport to the other side
  • Note: Arrow keys only work when the game board is focused (click on it first)
